\# Book



This folder contains the structured conceptual basis for the projected book derived from the doctoral thesis and its broader theoretical development.



\## Purpose

The purpose of this section is not to present a finished book manuscript, but to define its intellectual scope, thematic structure, and relationship with the scientific and conceptual work developed in the doctoral process.



\## Main file

\- `book\_outline.md`



\## Scope

The book outline should define:

\- general purpose,

\- intended audience,

\- conceptual core,

\- tentative chapter structure,

\- relation to the thesis and TDQ-based developments,

\- current development status.



\## Relationship with the thesis

This book is projected as a broader and more interpretive product derived from the doctoral work on wind-energy modeling, physical understanding, uncertainty, decision-oriented interpretation, and the emerging TDQ framework.



\## Repository role

This folder functions as the documentary bridge between the doctoral thesis and a future book-length scientific and conceptual dissemination product.
