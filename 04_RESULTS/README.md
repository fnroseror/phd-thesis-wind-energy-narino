# 04_RESULTS

## Purpose

This folder contains the structured outputs generated by the scientific workflow of the doctoral thesis.

It organizes the main computational results derived from preprocessing, physical characterization, forecasting models, uncertainty analysis, FNRR estimation, and energy projection.

---

## Structure

### `01_physical_characterization/`
Outputs associated with the physical and statistical characterization of the wind-related variables.

### `02_model_comparison/`
Comparative outputs of classical, machine learning, deep learning, and hybrid models.

### `03_pi90_uncertainty/`
Outputs related to prediction intervals, uncertainty quantification, and empirical PI90 coverage.

### `04_fnrr_outputs/`
Outputs associated with the FNRR indicator and its derived interpretation.

### `05_energy_projection/`
Outputs related to projected wind energy behavior and scenario-based estimation toward 2028.

### `06_extended_results/`
Additional structured outputs that support the thesis but are not central in the main manuscript.

---

## Scientific role

This folder acts as the structured evidence layer of the repository.

It separates generated results from code, figures, and tables in order to preserve clarity, traceability, and reproducibility.

---

## Closure criterion

This folder is considered complete when all outputs are organized by analytical function and can be traced back to the corresponding scripts in `03_CODE/`.
