# 06_TABLES

## Purpose

This folder contains the tabular material associated with the doctoral thesis.

Tables are organized by thesis chapter and supplementary support material.

---

## Structure

### `chapter_2/`
Tables associated with physical characterization and descriptive-inferential analysis.

### `chapter_3/`
Tables associated with forecasting models, metrics, and comparative performance.

### `chapter_4/`
Tables associated with the hybrid TDQ–PIESS framework, FNRR analysis, and energy projection.

### `supplementary/`
Additional tables required for technical support and extended reproducibility.

---

## Scientific role

This folder provides an ordered tabular layer that supports the interpretation of results and facilitates external academic review.

---

## Closure criterion

This folder is considered complete when all tables are classified consistently with thesis chapters and supplementary documentation.
