## 12. Scientific problem

The project addresses the study of wind speed and the inclusion of physical parameters for predicting electrical energy produced by wind sources.

The scientific problem is connected to:

- wind-speed behavior;
- meteorological variables;
- physical modeling;
- predictive modeling;
- wind-energy potential;
- uncertainty;
- and regional energy interpretation in Nariño.

---

## 13. General objective

To study wind speed and include physical parameters for the prediction of electrical energy produced by wind sources, with emphasis on the regional context of Nariño and the construction of a physical–statistical modeling framework.

## 15. Relationship with Wind Power Density

The project is connected to the physical construction of Wind Power Density:

```text
WPD = 0.5 · ρ · v³
```

This variable allows the project to connect wind-speed behavior with energetic interpretation.

Scientific role:

```text
Wind Power Density transforms wind-speed behavior into
a physically meaningful energy-potential variable.
```

---

## 16. Relationship with FNRR

The project is connected to the **Factor de No Regularidad Regional (FNRR)** as a structural descriptor of regional wind-energy irregularity.

Formal support:

```text
05_APPENDICES_SUPPORT/Anexo_G_Physical_Mathematical_Model_FNRR.md
```

The FNRR relationship supports the distinction between:

```text
free energy
```

and

```text
usable energy
```

through:

```text
E_usable = (1 − FNRR) · E_free
```

Repository interpretation:

```text
FNRR provides a structural interpretation of regional irregularity
and supports the transition from available energy to usable energy.
```

---
