\# Articles



This folder contains the structured conceptual basis for the three main derivative scientific articles projected from the doctoral thesis.



\## Purpose

The purpose of this section is not to present final submitted manuscripts yet, but to document the scientific direction, scope, and publication logic of the article portfolio derived from the thesis.



\## Planned article portfolio

\- `article\_01\_concept\_note.md`

\- `article\_02\_concept\_note.md`

\- `article\_03\_concept\_note.md`



\## Scope

Each concept note should define:

\- tentative title,

\- scientific problem,

\- objective,

\- expected contribution,

\- thesis sections or repository evidence supporting the paper,

\- suggested target journal or publication type,

\- current development status.



\## Relationship with the thesis

These articles are derived from the doctoral work on wind-speed-related physical factors and hybrid physical-statistical prediction of wind energy potential in Nariño, Colombia.



\## Repository role

This folder functions as the documentary bridge between the doctoral thesis and its derivative publication strategy.
