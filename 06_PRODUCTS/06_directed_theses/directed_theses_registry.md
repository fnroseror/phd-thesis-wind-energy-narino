# Directed Theses Registry

## Purpose

This file registers directed theses and supervised academic works associated with the doctoral research of **Favio Nicolás Rosero Rodríguez**.

The registry supports the academic traceability of student research projects derived from, connected to, or conceptually aligned with the doctoral thesis:

**“Estudio de la velocidad de viento e inclusión de parámetros físicos para la predicción de energía eléctrica producida por fuentes eólicas”.**

The projects listed in this registry involve student academic work.

Therefore:

- student authorship must be respected;
- project status must be reported honestly;
- the advisor role must not be confused with sole authorship;
- and institutional evidence should be handled responsibly.

This registry exists to document supervision and academic projection, not to appropriate student work.
