\# Directed Theses



This folder contains the documentary basis for undergraduate or graduate theses directed by the author and conceptually connected to the doctoral work.



\## Purpose

The purpose of this section is to preserve the academic projection of the doctoral thesis into supervised student work and to document those connections in an organized way.



\## Main file

\- `directed\_theses\_registry.md`



\## Scope

This section should document:

\- title of the directed thesis,

\- student or students,

\- academic level,

\- relationship with the doctoral work,

\- current development status,

\- relevant notes if needed.



\## Relationship with the thesis

These directed works are considered derivative or associated academic products when they inherit concepts, methods, themes, applications, or interpretive lines from the doctoral research.



\## Repository role

This folder functions as the documentary bridge between the doctoral thesis and its supervised academic extension through directed student projects.
