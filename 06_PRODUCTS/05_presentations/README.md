\# Presentations



This folder contains the documentary basis for presentations and academic dissemination products derived from the doctoral thesis.



\## Purpose

The purpose of this section is to preserve the presentation-oriented projection of the doctoral work and to organize its documentary support in a clear and traceable way.



\## Main contents

\- `presentations\_index.md`

\- `certificates/`



\## Scope

This section should document:

\- academic events or presentations linked to the thesis,

\- title or topic presented,

\- date or event context,

\- available support certificates,

\- relation between the presentation and the doctoral research.



\## Relationship with the thesis

These presentations are derived from the scientific development, dissemination, and academic projection of the doctoral thesis and its associated research products.



\## Repository role

This folder functions as the documentary bridge between the doctoral thesis and its academic dissemination history.
