# 05_presentations

## Purpose

This folder contains the documentary basis for presentations, academic dissemination products, and conference participation derived from the doctoral thesis:

**“Estudio de la velocidad de viento e inclusión de parámetros físicos para la predicción de energía eléctrica producida por fuentes eólicas”.**

The purpose of this folder is to preserve the dissemination trajectory of the doctoral work through academic presentations, conferences, institutional events, and scientific communication activities.

This folder is part of:

```text
06_PRODUCTS/
```

and should be interpreted as the academic dissemination evidence layer of the doctoral repository.

## 4. Relationship with the doctoral thesis

These presentations document the academic dissemination trajectory of the doctoral thesis.

The relationship can be summarized as:

```text
doctoral research
→ preliminary scientific communication
→ conference presentations
→ institutional and external dissemination
→ mature doctoral contribution
```

The earliest presentations focus on:

- wind speed,
- physical parameters,
- wind-energy prediction,
- and artificial intelligence applied to the physical phenomenon of wind.

The later presentation focuses on:

- predictive analytics,
- regional wind-energy potential,
- physical–statistical modeling,
- and meteorological data.

This evolution reflects the scientific maturation of the doctoral work.
